Transcript of 10th Battallion The Rifle Brigade, War Diary July to October 1915

July 21st 3:30pm the Battalion was posted to France, leaving it's base at Canadian Camp Larkhill, less transport, in half Battn, entraining at Jamesbury Station for Folkestone in two train loads, then on to Boulogne. disembarked and marched to rest camp
