"K" Camp Poheunghe May 1 Usual training and classes in bombing, bayonet fighting etc. continued.
Classes in Bayonet fighting of teams for Divisional competition commenced.
May 2 Detachment of men on working party at Brielen returned to Battalion. Work was continued on practice trenches being dug around the camp for bayonet fighting. Employed men drilled under Adjutant and R.S.M.
May 3 Usual Parades . Party paraded for work on new Railway.
May 6 and 7 Move to Calais. The Battalion, including all transport etc. evacuated "K" Camp at midnight and marched via Poheringhe to Roupoutre siding, where it entrained at 4.15am. for Calais. The Battalion detrained at Les pontinettes siding, marching via Dunkirk Road, to No. 6 Large Rest Camp. Beau nasins Entrainment took 55 minutes - Detrainment 70 minutes.

LEDRINGHEM May 20 Battalion moved from Ledringhem to "N" Camp at Poperinghe. This march although only 13miles was done in extremely hot weather. One man fell out.
"N" Camp May 21 Battalion moved from "N" Camp to billets in Poperinghe - Brigade Reserve. 20th Division relieved Guards Division in forward area.
Poperinghe May 20 to 26th At drill daily till 11am Rapid loading and bayonet fighting specially practiced. Blankets were disinfected.
May 26 Battalion moved by train from Poperinghe to Ypres. The relief of the 7th K.O.Y.L.I. was completed with out a casualty by 10.30pm. Fairly comfortable billets for the whole battalion. Battalion now in Brigade reserve to 61st Brigade.

Ypres May 27 A number of shells fell into Ypres in close proximity to our billets. Little damage however was done. Eight Officers and 280 men on working party during night, unloading transport, wiring at St. Jean and La Brique and work on Hooge Salient.
" May 28 Church parade in prison during morning. Every available man in the Battalion paraded at 8.15pm for digging borrow ditch in front line defences. Casualties 6 wounded one man remaining with unit. Enemy shelled Ypres between 9.30 and 10pm. Evidently an effort to find transport. No damage, however, was done.
" May 29 Ypres shelled spasmodically during early morning. Aeroplane activity on the part of the enemy. A German aeroplane was brought down behind the German lines by our Anti-aircraft guns. Working party employed on borrow ditch as on previous night.
May 30 Working party on front line borrow ditch at night.
" May 31 Our Aeroplanes were very active over the enemy lines. Enemy dropped a few shells into Ypres during the morning.
