"K" Camp Poheunghe May 1 Usual training and classes in bombing, bayonet fighting etc. continued.
Classes in Bayonet fighting of teams for Divisional competition commenced.
May 2 Detachment of men on working party at Brielen returned to Battalion. Work was continued on practice trenches being dug around the camp for bayonet fighting. Employed men drilled under Adjutant and R.S.M.
May 3 Usual Parades . Party paraded for work on new Railway.
May 6 and 7 Move to Calais. The Battalion, including all transport etc. evacuated "K" Camp at midnight and marched via Poheringhe to Roupoutre siding, where it entrained at 4.15am. for Calais. The Battalion detrained at Les pontinettes siding, marching via Dunkirk Road, to No. 6 Large Rest Camp. Beau nasins Entrainment took 55 minutes - Detrainment 70 minutes.
