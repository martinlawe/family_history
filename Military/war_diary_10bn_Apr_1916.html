G Camp April 1 Coy. Training carried out
April 2 Major W.V.L.PRESCOTT-WESTCARR joined and assumed command of Battn. Coy. training continued.
April 3 Inspection o f Battn. by C.O classes of instruction for N.C.O.'s commenced under B.S.M.
April 4 Training of N.C.O.'s and Coy. programme continued.
April 5 Miniature rage alloted to Battn. bad and indifferent shots and drsft were given preference for practice.
April 6 Coy. programme and preferences for practice.
April 7 Usual programme during morning.
At night Battn. moved to CHATEAU TROIS TOURS becoming No. 4 Battn.
