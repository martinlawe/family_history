AUTHIE 1/2/16 Working parties , 25 from each Company, were found for Hurdle Making
The rest of the Battalion practiced attack in the morning
Weather:- a fine and cold day 		Capt. M. SIMMONDE 36th Horse, Indian Coy. joined the Battalion for a week & was posted to "C" Company
do 2/2/16 The 145th Brigade relieved the 144th Brigade in the usual order and at the same lines - B and D Companies to Front Line A Company to the Keep, C Company to the Chateau.
Working parties were found in the evening on all the communication Trenches
A Conference of Company Officers was held at Brigade Headquarters at 4.30pm.
The enemy Guns were quiet, Except for some shelling in the afternoon but their Machine Guns were much more active.
Weather :- a fine day
