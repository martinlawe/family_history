CUL-DE-SAC FARM
SAILLY SUR-LYS		 January 1st to 9th Previous programme of training continued
Jan 10th Battn. commenced march into Reserve area in conjuction with remainder of Brigade. 1st day's march was to VIEUX BERQUIN and commenced at 10am, Destination was reached at 1.30pm (approx) and battn. went into billets for the night. Roads were good, weather fine and march was completed without stragglers.
VIEUX BERQUIN Jan 11th Battn. moved out at 9.45 am to complete march into new area. Destination - Canvas camp in C.16.a nr. SERCUE was reacted about 2.30pm.
Jan 12th to 14th Settling into camp which was in a rather dirty condition. Ground was very muddy and marshy. Drainage was commenced by digging small ditchesall over
SERCUE Jan 15th to 18th A programme of section, platoon and Coy drill was carried out.All junior N.C.O.'s and employed men were drilled by B.S.M. N.C.O.'s in communication and arm drill and employed men in open order skirmishing and arm drill.

SERCUE Jan 19th Draft of 20 O.R. arrived. In accordance with instructions received from 3rd corps a party of 500 Officers N.C.O's and men marched to the forest of NIEPPE at a point near LA MOTTE, for the purpose of cutting and bundling fascines to laydown in bad spots in the camp. Party was accompanied by Regt. Transport which brought the fascines back
Jan 20th Fascines laid and drainage system continued. Instruction in Bombing and Signalling and Coy programmes also continued.
Jan 21st The 59th Bde was inspected by Corps Commander (Lt Gen W. P. Pultanay) who in a short speech stated that the 20th Division was going to join another army in a new line and that he was sorry that the Division was leaving the 8th Corps.
Jan 22nd Battn. marched in conjuction with remainder of Bde to join 2nd Army at HARDIFRT, Coy's were billeted as follows -
A, I.29.b.6.6. B, J.25.a.3.3. C, I.25.b.1.5. D, I.30.c.4.5. with transport at I.25.b.5.5.3½. and Battn. H.Q. at I.35.C.6½.Y.

HARDIFORT Jan 23rd Settling in billets and Church parade for all denominations
Jan 24th to 26th 2nd Lt W.C.H.SANDERS joined battn. Previous programme of training continued
Jan 27th Draft of 20 O.R. received. An outpost scheme was carried out by the Battalion
Jan 28th and 29th Usual programme continued
Jan 30th Battalion route march
Jan 31st Brigade inspected by G.O.C. 2nd Army at a point between CASSEL and STEENVOPDE
