BELLEVUE_ PASSCHENDAELLE SECTOR 15-1-18 The Battalion relieved 2nd Bn.The Devon Regt. at BELLEVUE. Casualties Lt-Col J.J.B.Cole M.C. wounded by shell fire. Relief carried out in a heavy snow storm. Captain F.D.P.Milne took over command.
BELLEVUE_ PASSCHENDAELLE SECTOR 16-1-18 Battalion relieved the 1st Royal Irish Riffles in the line (left sub-sector).
Conditions of Post was extremely bad. Enemy activity slight. Casualties 6 O.Rs wounded. 7 O.Rs missing
