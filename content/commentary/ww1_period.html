World War 1 Period (1917 – 1918)

Although the First World War began earlier in Europe, the United States formally entered the conflict in 1917. Military mobilisation and wartime industry affected communities across the country.
Draft registration cards, military service records, and wartime employment records are important sources for tracing individuals during this short but significant period.
