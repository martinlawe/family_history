Second World War Period (1941 – 1945)

The United States entered the Second World War following the attack on Attack on Pearl Harbor in 1941. Wartime mobilisation transformed industry, employment, and family life across the country.
Military service records, draft registrations, factory employment records, and wartime newspapers become major sources for this period. Many women entered industrial employment for the first time during the war years.
