Progressive Era (1900 – 1917)

The Progressive Era brought political reform, labour movements, improved public health, and expanding educational opportunities. Technological advances, including electricity, automobiles, and telephones, changed everyday life for many Americans.
Genealogical records become increasingly detailed during this period, including census records, military registrations, newspapers, school records, and urban directories. Internal migration between rural and urban areas also increased significantly.
