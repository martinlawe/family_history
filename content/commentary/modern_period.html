Civil Rights and Modern America (1960 – 1980)

This period includes the Civil Rights Movement, social reform, cultural change, and continued technological development. Major political events, changing employment patterns, and increased mobility shaped modern American society.
Family historians may rely more heavily on newspapers, local records, oral history, photographs, and private documents due to increasing privacy restrictions on official records.
