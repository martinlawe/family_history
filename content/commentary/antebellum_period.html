Antebellum Period (c.1850 – 1861)

The Antebellum period refers to the years before the American Civil War, particularly in the southern states. The United States experienced rapid expansion westward, increasing industrialisation in the north, and growing tensions over slavery and states’ rights.
This period saw major migration into new territories and expanding railroad networks. Census records, land records, immigration documents, and local newspapers become increasingly important for tracing American families during this era.
