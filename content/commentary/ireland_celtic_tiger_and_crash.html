Celtic Tiger and Crash (1998–2010)

Overview: Ireland experienced rapid economic growth (1995–2008), dubbed the “Celtic Tiger.” Immigration increased for the first time in centuries. The global financial crash of 2008 brought severe economic hardship. County Kilkenny: The building boom led to housing developments in and around Kilkenny City. The crash affected rural construction and employment. Some emigration returned.
