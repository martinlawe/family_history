USA Civil War Period (c.1861 – 1865)

The American Civil War was fought between the Union and the Confederacy and had a profound effect on families across the country. Millions served in military units, while civilians experienced disruption, economic hardship, and political division.
Military service records, pension applications, draft registrations, and casualty records are major genealogical sources for this period. The war also resulted in significant migration and social change throughout the United States.
