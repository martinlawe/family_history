Family History

Elizabeth Brocas

1429–1475


Overview


Elizabeth Brocas was the daughter of William Brocas and Joanna Sandys. She is believed to be a granddaughter of Sir Bernard Brocas the younger. Her life marks an important generational link in the Brocas lineage, potentially connecting to the Moore and Doyley families.

Marriage and Children

Married Richard Moore (1426–1462).

Known child: Isabella Moore (1448–1478).

Timeline

1429: Birth in England.
c. 1450s: Marriage to Richard Moore.
1448: Birth of daughter Isabella.
1475: Death.

Historical Context

Elizabeth lived during the Wars of the Roses, a turbulent period in English history marked by dynastic conflict between the houses of Lancaster and York. While her role in this conflict is undocumented, noble families like the Brocases were often deeply entangled in court politics.
