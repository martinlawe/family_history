Family History

The aim of this page is to try to show how the Lawrence Family, from Walter Lawrence, evolved. This has not been an easy page to create, but hopefully it will provide a lot of information that will enable you, the reader, to understand the complexities.The diagram below, shows the two families, on the left is the American family group, the right shows the UK family group. both families are in generational order, which hopefully will provide some clarity. Note, this is the only diagram, that includes living family members, again with the aim to provide some clarity into generational order, the links for these family members are totally inactive, so whilst the view may show a link there is not an active link.

It is not known whether the American side of the family are aware of the UK side, logically it is believed that they do not, as the only census report showing that Walter is missing from is the 1920 US census, and that could have been for a number of reasons, none of which would point to a UK family. As a point, it has taken over twenty years to get to this point, my only points of reference were the knowledge that Walter had an american accent, which I got from Evelyn, and some idea that there were family members in Watford. My current understanding, as at September 2025, is that Jeffery Aliano is still living in Bristol, Hartford, Connecticut. But I have no intension to make contact, it would be too difficult to try to explain, as he is too far away, generationally, from Walter Lawrence.
