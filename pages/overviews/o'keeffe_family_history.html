O'Keeffe Family History

🧭 The O’Keeffe Family in Thomastown

The O’Keeffe family has long been associated with Thomastown, County Kilkenny. While some figures included below are part of ongoing research, others are firmly rooted in the documented family line. This section reflects both the broader historical context and the direct lineage.

🧬 Historical Figures (Under Review)

  • Richard O’Keeffe (c.1857–1945): Born in Old Court, Inistioge, near Thomastown, Richard was the son of John O’Keeffe and Catherine Lanigan. He married Judith Johanna Murphy in 1882 and had children including John Joseph and another Richard. He died in Tickerlivan, Graiguenamanagh. — This connection remains under research.
  • Patrick O’Keeffe (d.1860): Commemorated on a gravestone in the Chapel Lane Catholic Old Cemetery in Thomastown. He died on 25 May 1860 at the age of 66. — Further genealogical links are being explored.
