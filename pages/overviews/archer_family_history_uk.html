This part of the family becomes quite confusing, I'll explain- Matilda Fowler had two daughters Minnie and Emily, both by the same father, but outside of wedlock, Matilda, with the two girls, moved to Windsor, where she met and married Thomas Archer. Thomas adopted the two girls, hence the name change to Archer. According to the 1891 census report, both girls had the surname Fowler

Due to the adoption, I have not traced the Archer family back any further. Nor have I investigated Thomas any further.
