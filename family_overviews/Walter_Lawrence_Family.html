Family History

The aim of this page is to try to show how the Lawrence Family, from Walter Lawrence evolved. This has not been an easy page to create, but hopefully it will provide a lot of information that will enable you, the reader, to understand the complexities.The diagram below, shows the two families, on the left is the American family group, the righ shows the UK family group. both families are in generational order, which hopefully will provide some clarity. Note, this is the only diagram, that includes living family members, again with the aim to provide some clarity into generational order.

It is believed that the American side of the family are not aware of the UK side, logically this makes sense, as the only census report that Walter is missing from is the 1920 US census, and that could have been for a number of reasons, none of which would point to a UK family. As a point, it has taken over twenty years to get to this point, my only points of reference were the knowledge that Walter had an american accent, which I got from Evelyn, and some idea that there were family members in Watford. My current understanding, as at September 2025, is that Jeffery Aliano is still living in Bristol, Hartford, Connecticutt. But I have no intensions to make contact, it would be too difficult to try to explain, as he is too far away, generationally, from Walter Lawrence.
