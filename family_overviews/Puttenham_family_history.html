William Bold (c.1306 – 1376) Married Sibilla de Houghton (1329). This marriage ties the Bold family of Bold, Lancashire, with the Houghton family of Hoghton Tower.

Sir Richard Bold (1342 – 1390) Married Ellen Molyneux (1355). This is the pivotal link: the Bolds joined with the powerful Molyneux family of Sefton.

John de Bold (1360 – 1436) Married Emma de Ireland (1380, Bold, Lancashire). The Ireland family were another old Lancashire gentry house.

Sir Richard Bold (1380 – 1432) Justice of North Wales. Married Lady Elena de Halsall (1400, Hutton, Lancashire).

Sir Henry de Bold (1401 – 1465) Married Dulcia Savage (1422, Clifton, Cheshire). The Savages were a distinguished Cheshire family.

Ralph Goody (1420 – 1465) Married Ruth Bold (1444, Hertfordshire). This is where the Goody line intersects with the Bold family again.

John Puttenham (1450 – 1523) Married Agnes Ann Goody (1480, Edlesborough, Buckinghamshire).

Edward Puttenham (1505 – 1561) Married Ann Hampton (1535, Lode, Cambridgeshire).

Elizabeth Puttenham Married John Saunders (1535 – 1615, Amersham, Buckinghamshire).

Richard Saunders(1555 - ) Married Dorothy Symonds St Mary the Virgin's church, Amersham

John Saunders (1583 - 1634) Married Susanna Doyley 14th February 1605 linking the Doyley family to the Bold Family
