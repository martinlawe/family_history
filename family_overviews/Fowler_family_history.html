Family History

The above is the current tree, stretching back to William Munday 1731, it is believed that his mother was Sausanna Sirat, but as of October 2025, we cannot confirm this. William Munday married Elizabeth Allen in 1755, their son Wiliam Munday 1758 married Susanna Cook in 1778, and their daughter Hannah Munday 1787 married William Fowler 1805. Their son Joseph Fowler 1808 married Eliza Sinfield in 1830 and their Edward Fowler 1843 married Emma Spiers in 1848.

At this point we start to get close to known relatives, So, Edward and Emma had a daughter Matilda, born in 1868, The story from this point is somewhat complex, so I will not being going into that here, at this point in time. Matilda had two daughters Minnie and Emily, in 1888 and 1890 respectivley, she then Married Thomas Archer, and had a number of children with him, Thomas adopted both earlier daughters.
