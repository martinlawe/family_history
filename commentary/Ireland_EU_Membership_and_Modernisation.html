EEC/EU Membership and Modernization (1973–1998)

Overview: Joining the EEC (later EU) brought funding and infrastructure improvements. Education, health care, and women’s rights advanced. However, the Troubles in Northern Ireland overshadowed politics. County Kilkenny: Investment improved rural roads and farms. Heritage tourism expanded with attention to Kilkenny Castle and St. Canice’s Cathedral. Small industry and crafts (like pottery and design) flourished.
