Family History

This family history project began with the goal of preserving our shared past — not just names and dates, but the stories, decisions, and circumstances that shaped each generation. It draws on data compiled in RootsMagic, family letters, oral recollections, and wider historical research from local and national archives. Some family lines trace back centuries, touching on nobility, migration, and military service. Others are more modest, telling quiet stories of working lives, local ties, and resilience across difficult times. This site aims to bring them all together in one place.

This page provides a view of the family names that we have data for, by clicking on a button, you will be taken to that family history, some may take you to an "Under Construction" page, as further information is gathered.

Throughout this site, the theme colour will change in accordance to the family group that you are looking at, so for the UK families the colour will be blue, for the Irish family group it will be green,and for the American family, which does not appear readily, the colour will be maroon.

Privacy Notice: For privacy reasons, the website does not include details of living individuals.
