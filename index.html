Family History

This family history project began with the goal of preserving our shared past â€” not just names and dates, but the stories, decisions, and circumstances that shaped each generation. It draws on data compiled in RootsMagic, family letters, oral recollections, and wider historical research from local and national archives. Some family lines trace back centuries, touching on nobility, migration, and military service. Others are more modest, telling quiet stories of working lives, local ties, and resilience across difficult times. This site aims to bring them all together in one place.

By clicking on a button, above, you will be taken to that family history. The top row of buttons, the large ones, group the families together. The next row, which are clickable, are the main families within that group. the buttons stacked below are the main families for which information is available. Some buttons will take you to an "Under Construction" page, which means that the detail for that particular family, is not available, currently. The buttons an the left, give some General Historical Period information, again some may take you to an "Under Construction" page, as further information is gathered.

Privacy Notice: For privacy reasons, the website does not include details of living individuals.
