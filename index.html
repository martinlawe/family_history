This family history project began with the goal of preserving our shared past—not just names and dates, but the stories, decisions, and circumstances that shaped each generation. It draws on data compiled in RootsMagic, family letters, oral recollections, and wider historical research from local and national archives.

Some family lines trace back centuries, touching on nobility, migration, and military service. Others are more modest, telling quiet stories of working lives, local ties, and resilience across difficult times. This site aims to bring them all together in one place.

Pages include links to individual profiles, each offering further detail—birthplaces, spouses, children, occupations, and relevant historical context.

Not all histories are complete. Sections marked in grey are still in progress and will be updated as more information becomes available.

You can return to this homepage at any time using your browser's Back button or by using the Home link above.
